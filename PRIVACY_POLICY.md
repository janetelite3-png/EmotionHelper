# Privacy Policy for EmotionHelper

**Last Updated: November 13, 2025**

## Overview

EmotionHelper is committed to protecting your privacy. This app is designed with privacy-first principles for accessibility purposes.

## Data Collection

**We DO NOT collect, store, or transmit ANY personal data.**

Specifically:
- ❌ No images are saved
- ❌ No facial data is stored
- ❌ No emotion detection results are recorded
- ❌ No analytics or tracking
- ❌ No user accounts or login
- ❌ No internet connection required

## Permissions

The app requests only one permission:

### Camera Permission
- **Purpose**: To capture facial images for real-time emotion detection
- **Usage**: Images are processed instantly on your device and immediately discarded
- **Storage**: No images are saved to device storage or cloud services

## On-Device Processing

All emotion detection occurs entirely on your device using:
- Google ML Kit (on-device models)
- Local processing only
- No data leaves your device

## Third-Party Services

EmotionHelper does NOT use:
- Analytics services
- Advertising networks
- Cloud storage
- External APIs (except local ML Kit models)

## Children's Privacy

This app is safe for children and complies with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) as it collects no data whatsoever.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We will update this policy if our practices change. Check this document periodically.

## Contact

Questions about privacy? Open an issue on our GitHub repository.

## Consent

By using EmotionHelper, you agree to this privacy policy.
